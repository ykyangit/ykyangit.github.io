Jenkins 설치

brew install jenkins-lts

brew services stop jenkins-lts

brew remove jenkins-lts

rm -rf /Users/yyk/.jenkins

brew services start jenkins-lts

경로 찾기

readlink /usr/local/bin/jenkins

포트 변경


cd /usr/local/Cellar/jenkins-lts/2.361.4
vi ./homebrew.mxcl.jenkins.plist
--httpPort=8080 에서 포트 부분만 변경

빌드경로

.jenkins/workspace/

WAR 빌드 경로

/usr/local/Cellar/jenkins/2.309/libexec/jenkins.war

최초 설치시 password

cat /Users/yyk/.jenkins/secrets/initialAdminPassword
